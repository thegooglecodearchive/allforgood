Adding the All for Good Gadget to Google Sites

Adding the All for Good gadget to your Google Site is easy. First, log in to your account at Google Sites and open the site you want to add the gadget to.

Click on Edit Page in the top right corner of the page, and then go to the Insert menu on the top left. Click on More gadgets... at the end of the menu.

Click Add Gadget by URL.

Copy the text in the box below and paste it into the text box on the page.

Now click Add.

You can enter various options for the gadget on this screen, including the number of listings to display, the default location, and a default search term. Once you're done, click OK.

Make sure to click Save in the top right to finish your work.

That's it! You'll see the All for Good gadget on your site.
