Adding the All for Good Gadget to Gmail

Adding the All for Good gadget to your Gmail account is easy. First, log in to your account at Gmail.com

Click on Settings in the top right corner of the page.

Click on Labs in the orange bar.

Scroll all the way to the bottom of the page, and click Enable next to Add any gadget by URL. Then click the Save button.

You'll be back in your Inbox. Click on Settings in the top right again, and click on Gadgets in the orange bar. Copy the text in the box below and paste it into the text box on the page.



Now click Add.

That's it! You'll see the All for Good gadget in your sidebar.
