All for Good Apps

All for Good is more than just this web site – it's an open platform for finding and sharing volunteer activities across the web. Here are some of the apps developers have made to let you find and do good on your home page, your own web sites, your mobile device, and more. If you're interested in developing apps of your own, check out the API.

Create the Good Widget

The AARP has created Create The Good on the AFG platform. It allows you to search for ways to get involved or post your own opportunities. With their widget, you easily can add Create The Good to your own site.

the widget at CreateTheGood.org »

Independence Day Widget

Celebrate Independence Day through service. The Corporation for National and Community Service has created a simple widget to make it easy to find opportunities to contribute to your community on the 4th of July or any day of the year. You can find it on Serve.gov or add it to your website

the widget at Serve.gov »

Earth Day Widget

Change the world by serving it. You can celebrate Earth Day every day by finding relevant ways to make a difference in your local community. Use this widget developed by the Corporation for National and Community Service to learn how to do so.

the widget at Serve.gov »
